Data Deletion Instructions

Polyglot Pro does not store any personal data on our servers.

If you signed in with Facebook and wish to delete your account data, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account Settings.
  2. Click on “Apps and Websites”.
  3. Find “Polyglot Pro” and remove it.
