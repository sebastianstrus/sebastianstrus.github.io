Privacy Policy for Nobel Math

Effective Date: May 17, 2025

At Nobel Math, we respect and protect the privacy of our users, especially children under the age of 13. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect it.

Information We Collect

Nobel Math does not collect any personal information, such as name, email, or location, from users. We do not use any third-party services or analytics tools to track user activity.

Children’s Privacy

Nobel Math is designed for children under the age of 13, and we do not collect any personal information from children. We do not ask for any identifiable information from children, and we do not share any data with third parties.

Use of the App

Nobel Math is a learning tool designed to help children learn Math. The app does not collect or store any personal information or data related to the usage of the app.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any significant changes, we will notify users by updating the effective date above.

Contact Us
