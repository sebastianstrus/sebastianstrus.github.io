Privacy Policy for Nobel Stories

Effective Date: September 8, 2025

At Nobel Stories, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to not collecting any user information.

Information We Do Not Collect

Nobel Stories is designed to be a completely private and secure experience. We do not collect, use, save, or share any personal information from users. This includes, but is not limited to, names, email addresses, location data, photos, or any other personally identifiable information. The app does not collect any data or usage logs whatsoever.

Children’s Privacy

Our app is intended for children and is designed with their privacy as a top priority. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we do not collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. We do not ask for any identifiable information, and we do not share any data with third parties.

No Subscriptions or Advertisements

Nobel Stories is an ad-free application and does not offer any in-app subscriptions or purchases. There are no external links or third-party advertisements within the app.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any significant changes, we will notify users by updating the effective date above.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at feedback.nobelstories@gmail.com.
