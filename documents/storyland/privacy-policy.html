Last updated: May 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Storyland ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our iOS application. By using the App, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

Storyland is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

The App may store the following information locally on your device only:

The App offers in-app purchases processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

5. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information, the App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.
