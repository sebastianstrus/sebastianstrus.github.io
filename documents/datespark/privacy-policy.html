Privacy Policy

Welcome to DateSpark. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

DateSpark is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data generated within the app remains on your device.

Automatically Collected Information

DateSpark does not automatically collect any information about you or your device. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since DateSpark does not collect any personal information, we do not use, share, or process your data in any way. All content, preferences, and favorites you create within the app are stored locally on your device only.

3. Data Storage

All data in DateSpark is stored locally on your device using secure iOS storage mechanisms. This includes:

  • Your favorite questions
  • App preferences and settings
  • Language preferences
  • Onboarding completion status

This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties because we do not operate any servers or backend infrastructure.

4. Third-Party Services

DateSpark does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app functions entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to operate.

5. Children's Privacy

DateSpark is intended for users aged 4 and older and is designed to be safe for all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information at all, there are no privacy concerns related to children's data. All content remains on your device, and no information is ever transmitted or collected.

6. Data Security

Your data security is ensured by iOS's built-in security features. Since all data remains on your device and is never transmitted, the risk of unauthorized access is minimized. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device updated with the latest iOS version
  • Using device passcode or biometric authentication
  • Being cautious about who has physical access to your device

7. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: All your data is accessible through the app interface
  • Delete: You can delete the app at any time to remove all associated data
  • Modify: You can change your preferences and remove favorites at any time
  • Export: Currently, the app does not support data export, but since no data leaves your device, you maintain full control

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

9. International Users

DateSpark can be used worldwide. Since all data remains on your device, international data transfer regulations do not apply.
